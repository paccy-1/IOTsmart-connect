IOT INTRODUCTION

  1. Smart Homes: Smart appliances, thermostats, lights, and security systems can
    be controlled remotely and can learn your habits to optimize energy usage.
  2. Industrial Automation: Machines can communicate with each other and with humans
    in real-time, improving efficiency and reducing downtime.

IOT EXPLAINATION

  • Transportation: Vehicles can be connected to the internet, enabling features like
    navigation, traffic optimization, and autonomous driving.
  • Healthcare: Wearable devices and medical implants can monitor vital signs and transmit
    data to healthcare professionals for better diagnosis and treatment
